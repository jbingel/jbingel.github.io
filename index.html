// joachim bingel

I'm a second year PhD student at the CoAStaL NLP group, University of Copenhagen, where I'm advised by Anders Søgaard.

My research focuses on automatic text simplification. In particular, I investigate how texts can be adapted to match the needs of specific readers. Other research interests include machine learning (recently, multitask learning), using cognitive data to improve NLP systems, and corpus linguistics.

Before joining CoAStaL, I spent great years at the Institute for Computational Linguistics in Heidelberg, at Uppsala University, and at the Institute for the German Language (IDS Mannheim).
